Berlin experimental-music calendar: follow artists from echtzeitmusik.de, get reminders before shows, and build an artist catalogue.

Detailed Description:

A companion for echtzeitmusik.de — the calendar of Berlin's experimental, improvised, and new-music scene. See tonight's concerts in a single popup, follow the artists you care about, and let a quiet archive of the scene build itself as you browse.

FEATURES
• Tonight at a glance — browse concerts by day, week, month, or all time in a compact two-column popup.
• Follow artists — one tap, and a reminder lands before their next show (a day / a few hours / an hour ahead) via native notifications.
• Free-entry marker — shows with free admission are flagged automatically.
• Artist index — filter the whole programme by instrument or genre and see who's playing, where, and with whom.
• Your archive — every artist you come across is catalogued locally: instruments, venues, frequent collaborators, and full past & upcoming concert history.
• Calendar view — a clean month timeline of every date from the artists you follow.
• One-tap discovery — jump from any artist to Bandcamp, SoundCloud, or a web search.
• Export any event to your calendar (ICS).

All artist, instrument, and genre data is derived locally from the public echtzeitmusik.de calendar. Nothing is collected, transmitted, or shared — everything stays on your device.

NOTES FOR REVIEW
- Manifest V3.
- Permissions: storage, alarms, activeTab, scripting, notifications.
- Host access: https://echtzeitmusik.de/* only. Every network request goes there; nothing is sent out.
- No content scripts. After you follow an artist from the popup, activeTab + scripting is used once to show a brief in-page confirmation banner on the tab you were viewing — nothing is read from the page.
- Storage is local only (chrome.storage.local + a local IndexedDB catalogue).
- Privacy policy included (PRIVACY.md).
