⚡ ech wizard

Discover concerts from the Berlin experimental music calendar.

Data Source

All concert data comes from echtzeitmusik.de — the Berlin experimental music calendar maintained by the Berlin community. This extension fetches and analyzes the calendar to help you discover artists, instruments, and genres.

What it does

  • Shows today's concerts in a popup — with time, venue, and artist names
  • Follow artists and get notified 1 day, 5h, 3h, and 1h before their concerts
  • Explore instruments and genres with keyword filtering
  • See which artists play which instruments, ranked by frequency
  • Download ICS calendar files for any event

Privacy

All data stays in your browser. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Artist preferences and notification settings are saved locally using Chrome's storage.local API and never leave your device.

Concert data is fetched directly from echtzeitmusik.de and is not sent to any third party.
